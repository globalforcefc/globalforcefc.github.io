About Global Force FC

Our Story, Mission & Vision

Our Story

Global Force Football Club was established in 2025 by a group of passionate Nepali football enthusiasts working in Qatar. What started as casual kickabouts at Barwa Al Baraha has grown into a structured football community of 25+ dedicated players.

Based at the workers' accommodation ground in Barwa, we provide a platform for Nepali workers to showcase their talent, stay fit, and build lasting friendships through football. Despite our humble beginnings, we have already organized numerous tournaments and friendly matches against various clubs in Qatar.

Strength

Building physical and mental strength through disciplined training and competitive play.

Unity

Bringing together Nepali workers from all regions, united by their love for football.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in every match, training session, and tournament we participate in.
